Making spelling out of this world!



Does your child dread practicing for the spelling test?

RocketSpelling makes spelling out of this world! Children K - 12 will love blasting off to the moon and learning the whole way!

RocketSpelling Features

  • digital teacher speaks the word or sentence
  • hints
  • progress report
  • fun space theme
  • you can create unlimited word/sentence lists

How RocketSpelling Works

  • create the lists for your child
  • your child races to the moon and learns on the way
  • your child lands on the moon and sees a progess report
  • digital teacher encourages and congratulates your child

Hi! I'm your Digital Teacher and I'll speak to your child. I promise to motivate your child while making learning fun.

Once your child finishes spelling the word list, I'll present him with a progress report.

If she needs a little more practice, I'll let her try to spell only the words or sentences that she missed.
